NP Membership Benefits

This membership is exclusively for non-profit organizations and comes with no fee.

  • Online Directory Listing: Your organization will be listed in our public online directory.
  • Networking Events: Access to all Chamber-organized networking events and mixers.
  • Advocacy: Your voice contributes to the Chamber's advocacy efforts for local community development.
×

Bronze Membership Benefits

Designed for small and emerging businesses looking to establish their presence.

  • All NP Benefits: Includes directory listing and access to networking events.
  • Basic Directory Listing: Enhanced listing with contact information.
  • Member-Only Resources: Access to exclusive online business resources and guides.
  • Chamber Newsletter: Receive our weekly newsletter with local business news and opportunities.
×

Silver Membership Benefits

For growing businesses that desire more visibility and engagement.

  • All Bronze Benefits: Everything from the Bronze level.
  • Featured Directory Listing: Prominent listing in the online directory, including your company logo.
  • Event & Workshop Discounts: Significant discounts on Chamber-hosted events, workshops, and training sessions.
  • Advertising Opportunities: Access to discounted advertising rates in Chamber publications.
  • Mentorship Program: Opportunity to participate in our business mentorship program.
×

Gold Membership Benefits

Our premium level, offering maximum exposure and leadership opportunities.

  • All Silver Benefits: Enjoy all benefits from the Silver level.
  • Homepage Business Spotlight: Your business featured prominently on the Chamber's homepage.
  • Priority Sponsorship: Exclusive access and priority for sponsoring major Chamber events.
  • Executive Networking: Invitations to exclusive executive-level networking events.
  • Committee Participation: Opportunity to serve on Chamber committees and influence local policy.
  • Dedicated Support: Personalized support from Chamber staff for business development.
